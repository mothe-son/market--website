About Us

Welcome to the Datec Unitech Morobe Student Association. Our mission is to foster a community of IT enthusiasts and provide valuable resources and training.

Services

Technical Support We offer help desk services for troubleshooting hardware and software issues, along with on-site and remote support for users. Network Services Our team specializes in the design, implementation, and management of network infrastructure, including VPN and remote access solutions. Software Development We provide custom software solutions tailored to specific business needs, as well as mobile app development for various platforms. Web Development Our services include the creation and maintenance of websites and web applications, along with e-commerce solutions and content management systems. Database Management We handle the setup and administration of database systems, ensuring data backup, recovery, and performance optimization. Cloud Services We offer cloud computing solutions, including storage, computing power, and software as a service (SaaS), as well as migration services to transition to cloud environments. Cybersecurity Services Our cybersecurity services include security assessments and vulnerability testing, along with the implementation of security measures such as firewalls and antivirus software. IT Consulting We provide strategic advice on IT investments and infrastructure, along with guidance on best practices and compliance with regulatory requirements. Training and Development Our training services include workshops and sessions for staff on new technologies and software, as well as certification programs for IT professionals. Data Analytics We analyze data to provide insights that support decision-making and offer business intelligence solutions for reporting and visualization. Additionally, we facilitate opportunities for Papua New Guinea international students to study abroad at Chandigarh University in India, fostering collaboration and partnership for educational advancement. .

Membership

Datec Unitech Morobe Student Association Incorporation provides quality membership entry for students and employers involved in various activities related to Information Technology systems in Morobe Province and throughout Papua New Guinea. We aim to facilitate networking and collaboration among students and industry professionals, ensuring that our members have access to valuable resources and opportunities in the IT sector. Through our initiatives, we strive to enhance the educational experience and promote the growth of IT knowledge and skills within the community.Join us to gain access to exclusive resources and benefits. Become part of our growing community!
